ABOUT US


K.S MEMOIAL PUBLIC SCHOOL is located in the heart of the village along the Road & side in a modern spacious campus at which is eslily accessible form any part of the Sultanpur Village.

K.S MEMORIAL PUBLIC SCHOOL is a English-medium co educational institution Providing education based on the C.B.S.E. pattern run under the administration of Society Name.
The School is imparting junior High School level education through English medium with the aim of overall development of the students the vision is to provide the best of academic and extracurricular assistance to create intreset in learning understanding and include moral values,respect and honor towards the society at large.
